How It Works

From conversation to prediction
in four steps.

Crew replaces the survey form with a natural AI conversation — then does what no survey ever could.

01

AI Interviews, Not Surveys

Short, natural AI conversations replace static forms. Team members respond in their own words giving you qualitative depth and quantitative rigor at the same time.

02

Analyze and Identify Hidden Patterns

Responses are mapped to a science-backed framework of behavioral constructs. Not feelings — observable, measurable team behaviors.

03

Diagnose and Find What's Broken

The AI cross-references constructs, finds contradictions, and flags patterns the team can't see in itself. Named in plain language, instantly. The insight a consultant spends a day in interviews to find.

04

Predictions, Actions & Continuous Re-measurement

Leaders get what used to cost $500/hr: what the pattern means, where it leads, and exactly what to do first. Then Crew re-measures the effect — a continuous loop of team optimization, not a one-time report.
